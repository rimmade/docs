Syncly is most powerful when your whole team is working from the same customer intelligence. Inviting teammates means your customer success managers, product managers, support leads, and executives all have direct access to the same feedback data, dashboards, and AI-powered insights — without anyone waiting on a report from someone else. This guide walks you through inviting people, assigning roles, managing pending invitations, and removing members when needed.

## Invite Teammates

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Team Settings">
    Click your workspace name in the top-left corner of the Syncly sidebar, then navigate to **Settings → Team**. You'll see a list of everyone currently in your workspace along with their role and status.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Invite">
    Click the **Invite Team Member** button in the top-right corner of the Team page. A dialog box opens with the invitation form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter the Email Address">
    Type the work email address of the person you want to invite. You can invite multiple people at once by adding additional email addresses — press **Enter** or **comma** after each address to add the next one.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Assign a Role">
    Select a role for each invitee from the role dropdown:

    - **Admin** — full workspace control including settings, integrations, and billing
    - **Member** — full access to feedback, dashboards, AI features, and tag management
    - **Viewer** — read-only access to feedback and dashboards

    Not sure which role to choose? The [Workspace guide](/getting-started/workspace) has a complete permissions table to help you decide.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send the Invitation">
    Click **Send Invitation**. Each invitee receives an email from Syncly with a link to accept the invitation and create their account (or sign in if they already have one). The invitation link is valid for 7 days.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Manage Pending Invitations

Invitations you've sent but that haven't been accepted yet appear in the **Pending** section at the bottom of the **Settings → Team** page.

From the pending invitations list, you can:

- **Resend an invitation** — click **Resend** next to the invitee's email to send a fresh invitation email with a new 7-day link. Use this if the original email expired or wasn't received.
- **Cancel an invitation** — click **Cancel** next to the invitee's email to revoke the invitation. The link in their original email immediately becomes invalid.

## Update a Team Member's Role

You can change any team member's role at any time from the **Settings → Team** page:

1. Find the team member in the active members list.
2. Click the role badge next to their name.
3. Select the new role from the dropdown.

The role change takes effect immediately. The team member doesn't need to sign out and back in for the new permissions to apply.

## Remove Team Members

When someone leaves your team or no longer needs access to Syncly, remove them to keep your workspace secure and your seat count accurate.

1. Go to **Settings → Team**.
2. Find the team member you want to remove.
3. Click the **⋯** (more options) menu to the right of their name.
4. Select **Remove from Workspace**.
5. Confirm the removal in the dialog that appears.

Removing a team member immediately revokes their access to the workspace. Any dashboards, tags, or reports they created remain intact in the workspace — their work is not deleted.

<Note>
  The number of team members you can add to your Syncly workspace depends on your plan. Starter plans include a limited number of seats. Growth and Enterprise plans support larger teams, with Enterprise offering unlimited seats and custom user provisioning via SSO. If you reach your seat limit, you'll see a notice when you try to send a new invitation. Visit [Plans & Billing](/getting-started/plans-billing) to upgrade, or [contact sales](/support/contact) to discuss Enterprise options.
</Note>
