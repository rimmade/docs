Creating a Syncly account takes less than two minutes. Once you sign up, Syncly walks you through a short onboarding flow to set up your workspace and connect your first data source. This guide covers the full sign-up process, signing back in, enterprise single sign-on (SSO), and resetting your password.

<Note>
  All new Syncly accounts start with a free trial that gives you full access to the platform's core features. No credit card is required to begin. See [Plans & Billing](/getting-started/plans-billing) for details on what's included in your trial and how to upgrade when you're ready.
</Note>

## Sign Up for Syncly

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to syncly.app">
    Open your browser and navigate to [syncly.app](https://syncly.app). You'll land on the Syncly homepage.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Get a Demo or Start Free">
    Choose the option that fits your situation:

    - **Start Free** — creates your account immediately and drops you into the onboarding flow.
    - **Get a Demo** — schedules a guided walkthrough with the Syncly team. You'll receive account access after the demo session.

    If you're joining a workspace that a colleague already created, look for the invitation email they sent and click **Accept Invitation** instead of signing up from the homepage.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill In Your Company Details">
    Complete the sign-up form with the following information:

    | Field | Notes |
    |---|---|
    | Full name | Your first and last name |
    | Work email address | Use your company email — this becomes your login |
    | Company name | Used to name your Syncly workspace |
    | Company size | Helps Syncly suggest the right plan for your team |
    | Password | Minimum 8 characters; use a strong, unique password |

    Click **Create Account** when you're done.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Your Email Address">
    Syncly sends a verification email to the address you provided. Open the email and click **Verify Email Address**. The link expires after 24 hours, so verify promptly.

    If the email doesn't arrive within a couple of minutes, check your spam or junk folder. You can also request a new verification email from the sign-in page by clicking **Resend verification email**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign In to Your Account">
    After verifying your email, you're redirected to the Syncly sign-in page. Enter your email address and password, then click **Sign In**. Syncly takes you into your new workspace and begins the workspace setup wizard.

    On future visits, go to [syncly.app](https://syncly.app) and click **Sign In** in the top navigation to access your workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Single Sign-On (SSO)

Single sign-on is available on Syncly's Enterprise plan. SSO lets your team sign in to Syncly using your organization's existing identity provider — such as Okta, Azure Active Directory, or Google Workspace — without managing a separate Syncly password.

To enable SSO for your organization:

1. Sign in as a workspace Admin.
2. Go to **Settings → Security → Single Sign-On**.
3. Select your identity provider from the list.
4. Enter the metadata URL or upload the XML configuration file provided by your identity provider.
5. Click **Save and Enable SSO**.

Once SSO is active, members of your organization are redirected to your identity provider's login page whenever they access Syncly. Admins can choose to enforce SSO so that password-based sign-in is disabled for all members.

<Note>
  Contact the Syncly sales team to upgrade to an Enterprise plan and unlock SSO. → [Contact Sales](/support/contact)
</Note>

## Reset Your Password

If you forget your password or need to set a new one:

1. Go to [syncly.app](https://syncly.app) and click **Sign In**.
2. Click **Forgot password?** below the password field.
3. Enter your email address and click **Send Reset Link**.
4. Open the password reset email and click the link inside. The link is valid for 1 hour.
5. Enter and confirm your new password, then click **Update Password**.
6. Sign in with your new password.

If you don't receive the reset email, check your spam folder and confirm you're using the email address associated with your Syncly account. For further help, contact [/support/contact](/support/contact).
