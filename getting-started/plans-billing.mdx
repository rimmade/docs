## Upgrade or Downgrade Your Plan

### Upgrading

Upgrading gives you access to higher seat limits, more integrations, additional AI features, and expanded data retention immediately upon checkout.

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Upgrade Plan**.
3. Review the plan comparison and select **Growth** or contact sales for **Enterprise**.
4. Enter your payment details and click **Confirm Upgrade**.

Your new plan activates instantly. Syncly prorates the charge for the remainder of your current billing period — you only pay the difference between your old and new plan for the days remaining.

### Downgrading

You can downgrade your plan at any time, but note that downgrading reduces your available seats, integrations, and feature access.

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Change Plan**.
3. Select the lower plan tier and click **Confirm Downgrade**.

Your downgrade takes effect at the start of your next billing period. Until then, you retain access to your current plan's features. If your current usage (seats, integrations, feedback volume) exceeds the limits of the lower plan, Syncly will alert you during the downgrade flow so you can make adjustments before the change goes live.

<Warning>
  Downgrading to Starter removes access to integrations beyond the three included in that plan. Feedback from disconnected integrations is retained in Syncly, but new data will not sync until you reconnect those integrations on a Growth or Enterprise plan.
</Warning>

## Billing Cycle

Syncly offers two billing cycles:

- **Monthly** — You're charged at the start of each calendar month for the upcoming month. Monthly billing offers flexibility with no long-term commitment.
- **Annual** — You're billed once per year for 12 months of service. Annual billing includes a discount compared to monthly pricing (typically equivalent to two months free).

To switch between monthly and annual billing, go to **Settings → Billing → Billing Cycle** and select your preferred option. The change takes effect at the start of your next renewal date.

## Access Your Invoices

Syncly generates an invoice automatically for every charge — at the start of each billing cycle and for any mid-cycle upgrades.

To view and download your invoices:

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Invoices**.
2. Click any invoice in the list to open a detailed view.
3. Click **Download PDF** to save a copy for your records or to share with your finance team.

Invoices include your plan name, billing period, seat count, and a line-item breakdown of all charges.

## Update Your Payment Method

To update the credit or debit card on file:

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Payment Method**.
2. Click **Update Payment Method**.
3. Enter your new card details in the secure form.
4. Click **Save**.

Your new payment method is charged at your next renewal date. Syncly does not store raw card numbers — all payment data is handled securely through our payment processor.

If a payment fails, Syncly sends an email to the workspace Admin with instructions to update the payment method. Your workspace remains active during a brief grace period while the payment issue is resolved.
