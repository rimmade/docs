## What to Include When Contacting Support

Providing the right details upfront helps the support team diagnose and resolve your issue as quickly as possible. When you reach out, include the following:

- **Workspace name:** The name of your Syncly workspace (visible in the top-left of the app or under **Settings → General**).
- **Integration name:** If your issue involves a specific integration (e.g., Zendesk, HubSpot), name it explicitly.
- **Steps to reproduce:** A brief numbered list of what you did before the issue occurred. For example: *"1. Opened Feedback Hub. 2. Applied tag filter 'Billing'. 3. Clicked Export → CSV. 4. Download failed with an error."*
- **Screenshots or screen recordings:** A visual of the error message or unexpected behavior saves significant back-and-forth. Attach screenshots directly to your email or paste them into chat.
- **Browser and device:** If the issue is visual or login-related, let us know which browser (and version) and operating system you're using.

<Note>
  For data deletion or privacy-related requests, please also include the email address associated with the data in question. See the [Data Privacy](/support/data-privacy) page for more on your rights as a customer.
</Note>

## Response Time Expectations

| Channel | Standard Response Time | Enterprise SLA |
|---|---|---|
| In-app chat | Within minutes (business hours) | Priority queue with faster SLA |
| Email | Within 1 business day | Within 4 business hours |
| Demo / Sales | Within 1 business day | Dedicated account manager |

Business hours are Monday through Friday, 9 AM – 6 PM (Pacific Time). Enterprise customers with an active SLA agreement receive prioritized responses and may have access to a dedicated support contact. Reach out to the sales team at [syncly.app/demo](https://syncly.app/demo) to learn more about enterprise support options.
