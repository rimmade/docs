Whether you're just getting started or exploring a specific feature, the answers below cover the questions Syncly users ask most often. If you don't find what you're looking for here, visit the [Troubleshooting guide](/support/troubleshooting) or [contact the support team](/support/contact) directly.

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="What is Syncly?">
  Syncly is an AI-native customer intelligence platform that centralizes all your customer feedback — from support tickets, surveys, sales calls, reviews, and more — and automatically surfaces the insights that matter. Using AI-powered auto-tagging, sentiment analysis, and the Hey Syncly AI chat interface, Syncly helps your team understand what customers are experiencing, prioritize issues, and make faster, more confident decisions. Syncly is backed by Y Combinator and trusted by customer-centric teams worldwide.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What data sources does Syncly support?">
  Syncly connects to 19+ integrations across support, CRM, surveys, sales, and communication tools. Current integrations include:

  - **Support & messaging:** Zendesk, Intercom, Gmail, Slack, Discord
  - **CRM & sales:** Salesforce, HubSpot, Gong, Grain
  - **Surveys:** SurveyMonkey, Typeform
  - **App stores & social:** Google Play, Syncly Social (video social listening)
  - **Productivity & automation:** Jira, Linear, Zapier
  - **Manual uploads:** CSV file imports

  You can connect multiple sources simultaneously, and Syncly unifies all incoming feedback into a single Feedback Hub.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How long does it take to set up?">
  Most teams are up and running within a single session. Connecting an integration — such as Zendesk or Intercom — takes just a few minutes using OAuth or API key authentication. Once connected, Syncly begins ingesting and tagging your historical and live data automatically. You'll start seeing insights in your Dashboard and Feedback Hub shortly after your first sync completes. For larger data volumes or enterprise rollouts, the Syncly team can assist you during onboarding.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How does AI auto-tagging work?">
  Syncly's AI reads each piece of incoming customer feedback and assigns one or more tags based on the topics, themes, and issues it detects. The model is trained to understand the language of customer feedback and maps content to your tag taxonomy automatically — no manual rules required. Tags appear on each feedback item in the Feedback Hub and are used to power trend detection, sentiment breakdowns, and reporting across your Dashboard. You can also customize the taxonomy to match your team's specific categories.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is my customer data secure?">
  Yes. Syncly protects your data with encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). Integration credentials such as OAuth tokens are stored in encrypted form and are never exposed in the interface or logs. Syncly undergoes regular third-party security audits and is built to enterprise-grade standards including SOC 2 compliance and GDPR readiness. For full details, see the [Security](/support/security) and [Data Privacy](/support/data-privacy) pages.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I customize the tag taxonomy?">
  Yes. While Syncly's AI generates tags automatically, you have full control over your tag taxonomy. You can create custom tags that reflect your product areas, team structure, or business priorities. Once you define a tag, the AI applies it to new and existing feedback going forward. You can also manually correct or reassign tags on individual feedback items, which helps the AI improve accuracy over time within your workspace.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What languages does Syncly support?">
  Syncly's AI processes customer feedback written in multiple languages, including English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Japanese, and more. Auto-tagging and sentiment analysis work across supported languages, so you can surface insights from a global customer base without translating feedback first. The Syncly interface itself is available in English. If you have specific language requirements, reach out to the [support team](/support/contact) to confirm coverage for your use case.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How is Syncly Social different from Syncly?">
  Syncly Social is a dedicated video social listening product that extends Syncly's AI intelligence to video content — think product reviews on YouTube, social media video posts, and video-based customer commentary. While core Syncly ingests text-based feedback from support, surveys, CRMs, and app stores, Syncly Social captures and analyzes spoken and visual customer sentiment from video sources. Both products share the same AI foundation and can feed insights into a unified view of your customer experience.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I export my data?">
  You can export feedback data as CSV and reports or dashboards as PDF directly from the Syncly interface. In the Feedback Hub, apply your desired filters and click **Export → CSV** to download matching feedback. From the Dashboard, use the **Export** menu to download a PDF snapshot of any widget or full dashboard view. For scheduled, automated exports, you can set up a Workflow using the Zapier integration. See the [Export Data](/support/export-data) guide for step-by-step instructions.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I connect Syncly to tools not on the integrations list?">
  Yes — in two ways. First, you can use the **CSV import** feature to manually upload feedback data from any tool that can export a spreadsheet. Second, Syncly's **Zapier integration** lets you connect to thousands of additional apps and automate data flows without writing code. If you have a high-volume or enterprise need for a native integration that isn't currently supported, contact the [Syncly team](/support/contact) to discuss your requirements — new integrations are added regularly based on customer demand.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens if I disconnect an integration?">
  When you disconnect an integration, Syncly stops pulling new data from that source. Your existing feedback data that was already ingested remains in your Feedback Hub and is unaffected — nothing is deleted automatically. If you reconnect the same integration later, Syncly will resume syncing from where it left off based on your configured data range settings. If you want to remove previously ingested data from a specific source, contact [Syncly Support](/support/contact) to request a data deletion.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I cancel my subscription?">
  You can manage your subscription from your workspace **Settings → Billing** page. If you want to cancel, you can initiate the process there or contact the Syncly support team directly via in-app chat or email. Syncly does not lock you in — your data remains accessible until the end of your current billing period. If you're canceling due to a specific issue or unmet need, the team would love to hear from you and may be able to help resolve it before you go.
</Accordion>
