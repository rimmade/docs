The Feedback Hub is the command center of your Syncly workspace. Instead of jumping between support inboxes, survey tools, review platforms, and spreadsheets, you get a single, continuously updated stream of every customer signal flowing into your account. From here you can read, filter, tag, and act on feedback without ever leaving the page — turning what used to be a fragmented research project into a focused daily practice.

## What the Feedback Hub Shows You

Every piece of feedback Syncly ingests from your connected integrations appears here the moment it arrives. Syncly automatically enriches each item with an AI-generated tag, a sentiment score, and source metadata, so by the time you open the Hub, the work of categorization is already done.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="All Feedback" icon="list-ul">
    A reverse-chronological feed of every feedback item across all sources. This is your default view and the best place to start your daily review.
  </Card>
  <Card title="By Source" icon="plug">
    Filter the feed to a single integration — for example, only Intercom conversations or only App Store reviews — so you can focus on one channel at a time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="By Tag" icon="tag">
    Group feedback by the AI-generated tags applied to each item. Use this view to see everything customers are saying about a specific product area, feature, or theme.
  </Card>
  <Card title="By Sentiment" icon="face-smile">
    Segment the feed into Positive, Neutral, and Negative buckets. Use this view to triage urgent dissatisfaction or to collect evidence of what customers love.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Filtering and Searching Feedback

Syncly gives you powerful controls to narrow the feed to exactly what you need.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Search">
    Use the **Search** bar at the top of the Feedback Hub to find feedback by keyword or phrase. Syncly searches the full text of every feedback item in real time. Wrap a phrase in quotation marks (e.g., `"slow load time"`) to match it exactly.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Filter Panel">
    Click **Filters** to open the filter panel. You can combine multiple filters at once:

    - **Date range** — restrict results to a specific period
    - **Source** — one or more connected integrations
    - **Tag** — one or more AI-generated or custom tags
    - **Sentiment** — Positive, Neutral, or Negative
    - **Customer segment** — any segment you have defined in Settings

    Active filters appear as chips below the search bar. Click the **×** on any chip to remove that filter.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Saved Views">
    Once you have a filter combination you use regularly, click **Save View**, give it a name, and it will appear in the left sidebar under **My Views**. Saved views update in real time as new feedback arrives — no manual refresh needed.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Marking Feedback as Reviewed or Actionable

Keeping the Feedback Hub tidy helps your team move quickly. Syncly gives you two lightweight status actions for every feedback item.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the feedback item">
    Click any item in the feed to expand its detail panel on the right side of the screen. You'll see the full text, source, timestamp, assigned tags, and sentiment score.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark as Reviewed">
    Click **Mark Reviewed** to acknowledge that you have read the item. Reviewed items dim in the feed by default, so unreviewed feedback always stands out. You can toggle the visibility of reviewed items using the **Show Reviewed** switch in the filter bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark as Actionable">
    Click **Mark Actionable** to flag feedback that requires follow-up — a bug to file, a feature request to log, or a customer to contact. Actionable items appear in the **Actionable** saved view in the sidebar, giving your team a built-in to-do list derived directly from customer voice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add an internal note">
    Use the **Note** field at the bottom of the detail panel to leave context for teammates. Notes are internal and are never shared with the customer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Accordion title="Bulk actions">
  Select multiple feedback items by checking the boxes on the left side of each row. Once you have a selection, the bulk action bar appears at the bottom of the screen. From there you can mark all selected items as Reviewed, mark them as Actionable, reassign tags, or export the selection to CSV.
</Accordion>

<Tip>
  Make reviewing the Feedback Hub the first five minutes of your workday. Open the **All Feedback** view filtered to the last 24 hours, scan for anything Actionable, and mark everything else as Reviewed. This daily habit keeps your team anchored to real customer voice and prevents important signals from getting buried.
</Tip>
