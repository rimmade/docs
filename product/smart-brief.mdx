Smart Brief is Syncly's AI-powered narrative engine that reads your dashboard charts and metrics and writes a concise, plain-language summary of what the data actually means. Instead of spending time interpreting graphs before a meeting or manually writing up a status recap, you get a ready-made executive summary at the top of every dashboard — updated automatically whenever your data refreshes. Smart Brief translates numbers into narrative so you can focus on action, not analysis.

## What Smart Brief Does

Smart Brief analyzes every chart and metric panel on your current dashboard and synthesizes the key signals into a coherent written summary. It identifies what's changed, what's notable, and where your attention is most needed — written in plain language that anyone on your team can understand, regardless of their data fluency.

## Where to Find Smart Brief

Smart Brief appears at the **top of every dashboard**, just above your first chart panel. Look for the **Smart Brief** card with the sparkle icon (✦). It loads automatically when you open a dashboard — no setup or configuration required.

If a dashboard is newly created and doesn't yet have enough data to generate a summary, Smart Brief will display a prompt to return once more data has been ingested.

## Regenerating a Smart Brief

Smart Brief updates automatically each time your connected data sources sync. If you want to force a refresh — for example, after triggering a manual data sync or making changes to your dashboard filters — click the **Regenerate** button in the top-right corner of the Smart Brief card. A new summary will generate within a few seconds based on the current state of your dashboard.

<Info>
Regenerating a Smart Brief does not affect your data or dashboard configuration. It simply re-runs the AI summarization on the current snapshot of your metrics.
</Info>

## Sharing a Smart Brief

You can share a Smart Brief in three ways directly from the Smart Brief card:

1. **Copy to clipboard** — Click **Copy** to copy the full summary text. Paste it into any document, message, or email.
2. **Send by email** — Click **Share via Email**, enter one or more recipient addresses, and send the summary with a link back to the dashboard.
3. **Send to Slack** — Click **Share to Slack** and select a channel or direct message recipient. The Smart Brief text posts as a formatted message with a dashboard link.

To access sharing options, click the **Share** button (arrow icon) in the top-right corner of the Smart Brief card.

## Smart Brief in One-Click Reports

When you generate a one-click report from any dashboard, Smart Brief is included automatically at the top of the exported document. You don't need to copy and paste — the narrative summary is embedded directly above the charts in the PDF or shareable report link, giving recipients immediate context before they review the data.

<Tip>
Smart Brief is perfect for weekly leadership standups. Open your main dashboard before the meeting, copy the Smart Brief summary, and paste it into your standup doc or slide deck as your data narrative. Your team gets a clear, jargon-free picture of customer health without needing to interpret charts in real time — and you save the time it would take to write the summary yourself.
</Tip>
