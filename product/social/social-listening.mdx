Your brand is being talked about right now in videos you've never seen. A creator mentions your product by name without tagging you. A reviewer holds your packaging up to the camera. A customer talks through their experience in a vlog. Traditional social listening tools miss all of it. Syncly Social's Social Listening feature captures brand mentions from audio, visuals, and text across 10+ platforms simultaneously — so you get a complete, unfiltered view of what your audience actually thinks about your brand.

## What Social Listening Monitors

Syncly Social tracks brand mentions across every layer of social video content:

- **Spoken words via Audio Intelligence** — Syncly transcribes video audio in real time, detecting brand names, product names, and custom keywords even when they're never typed or hashtagged.
- **Visual brand appearances via AI Vision** — logos, product packaging, and branded visuals that appear on screen are identified automatically, giving you visual mention data alongside audio and text.
- **Comments and reactions** — traditional text signals from comments sections, replies, and reaction posts are captured alongside video content for a full-spectrum view.
- **Multi-language content** — Global Insights ensures that brand conversations in any language are tracked and surfaced in your dashboard.

## The Intention vs. Perception Gap

One of the most valuable — and most overlooked — insights in brand management is the gap between what you *intend* your brand to communicate and how customers *actually* perceive it. Syncly Social's sentiment gap analysis makes this visible.

When your brand messaging emphasizes, say, premium quality, but social conversations trend toward "overpriced" or "not worth it," that gap is costing you conversions and loyalty. Syncly Social surfaces this mismatch by analyzing the sentiment and themes of organic brand conversations and comparing them against the narrative you're actively pushing.

Use the sentiment gap view to:

- Identify which product attributes customers praise versus criticize
- Spot messaging that resonates and double down on it in future campaigns
- Catch emerging negative perception trends before they compound into a larger reputation issue
- Align your CX team around the specific pain points audiences are expressing in video

## Understanding Your Social Listening Dashboard

Once your monitoring is active, the Social Listening dashboard gives you a real-time view of your brand's presence across platforms:

- **Mention volume** — see how many times your brand is mentioned across all platforms, broken down by day, week, or custom date range. Spot spikes that signal viral moments or emerging issues.
- **Sentiment trend** — track whether brand sentiment is moving positive, neutral, or negative over time. Drill into specific time periods to understand what drove a sentiment shift.
- **Platform breakdown** — understand which platforms are generating the most brand conversation and where your audience is most active.
- **Top content** — surface the highest-performing posts, videos, and creator content that mentions your brand.

## Conversation Insights

Conversation Insights goes deeper than mention counts. It analyzes the themes, topics, and patterns within audience discussions about your brand — identifying what people are talking *about* when they mention you, not just that they mentioned you.

Use Conversation Insights to:

- Discover recurring topics your audience associates with your brand
- Identify questions customers ask about your products in video comments
- Surface unexpected brand associations (positive or negative) you weren't aware of
- Understand the context around high-volume mention spikes

## Performance Monitor

Performance Monitor tracks how your own brand content performs over time across all connected platforms. Compare content formats, posting cadences, and topic areas to understand what consistently drives engagement with your audience. Use this data to inform your content strategy with evidence rather than intuition.
