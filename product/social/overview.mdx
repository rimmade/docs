Syncly Social is the first video-first social listening platform that goes beyond text. While traditional tools scan captions and comments, Syncly Social listens to what people *say* in videos and *shows* on screen — giving your brand a complete picture of how audiences talk about you across every major social platform. Whether you're a brand marketer running global campaigns or a CX manager tracking customer sentiment, Syncly Social surfaces the insights that text-only tools miss.

## What Makes Syncly Social Different

Most social listening tools were built for a text-first internet. They scan posts, captions, and hashtags — but they go silent the moment someone mentions your brand out loud in a TikTok, YouTube Short, or Instagram Reel. Syncly Social closes that gap with two core AI engines:

- **Audio Intelligence** — transcribes and analyzes spoken words in video content, so a creator saying your brand name on camera counts as a mention, even if they never type it.
- **AI Vision** — identifies visual brand appearances such as logos, products, and packaging that appear on screen, even when no text or speech accompanies them.

Together, these capabilities mean you monitor the full context of any brand conversation — not just the fraction that surfaces in text form.

Beyond video intelligence, Syncly Social gives you:

- **10+ social platforms** monitored from a single dashboard, including TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, X, and more.
- **90% faster setup** compared to traditional social listening tools — your data starts flowing within hours, not after months of onboarding and configuration.
- **Global Insights** with multi-language support so you can track brand conversations in any market without switching tools.
- **Ask Syncly AI** — a natural-language chat interface that lets you ask questions about your data and get plain-English answers instantly.
