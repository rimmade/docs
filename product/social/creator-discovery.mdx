The most effective influencer partnerships don't start with a cold pitch — they start with a creator who already believes in your brand. Syncly Social's Creator Discovery feature surfaces creators who are organically mentioning your brand, products, or relevant topics, so you can identify authentic advocates before you ever send a single outreach email. Instead of guessing which influencers might be a good fit, you start with evidence.

## What Creator Discovery Does

Creator Discovery continuously scans content across 10+ social platforms and identifies creators whose videos, audio, or visual content references your brand or product keywords. These creators have already decided to talk about you on their own — which means their audiences are seeing unsponsored, authentic content about your brand right now.

Beyond finding these creators, Syncly Social gives you an AI-powered analysis of each one so you can prioritize outreach based on what actually drives campaign results.

## Why Organic Creators Outperform Cold Outreach

When a creator already mentions your brand before any partnership exists, their audience sees that endorsement as genuine. Sponsored content from a creator with no prior brand connection often reads as transactional to their audience — and audiences are increasingly good at detecting that gap. Creators discovered through Syncly Social come with a built-in credibility signal: they chose to talk about your brand unprompted.

This translates directly to campaign performance. Organic advocates typically deliver higher engagement rates, more authentic audience responses, and stronger brand lift compared to influencers sourced through follower-count-first approaches.

## What Syncly Social Analyzes Beyond Follower Count

Follower count is one of the least predictive metrics for campaign success. Syncly Social evaluates each creator across a richer set of signals:

- **Audience demographics** — understand the age, location, and interests of a creator's actual audience using Demographic Analysis, so you can confirm they're reaching the people you want.
- **Engagement quality** — Syncly measures not just the volume of likes and comments, but the quality of audience interaction: are followers asking questions, sharing content, and responding substantively?
- **Brand alignment score** — an AI-generated score that reflects how well a creator's content, tone, and audience match your brand's identity and values.
- **Content relevance** — how frequently and in what context does the creator mention topics related to your brand, category, or products?
- **Audience reach and overlap** — understand total addressable reach and identify audience overlap between multiple creators to avoid redundancy in your influencer mix.

## How to Use Creator Discovery

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Creator Discovery in Syncly Social">
    Log in at [creator.syncly.app](https://creator.syncly.app) and navigate to the Creator Discovery section from the main sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter by platform, category, follower range, or engagement rate">
    Use the filter panel to narrow your search. You can filter by social platform, content category, follower range, minimum engagement rate, or geographic focus to match your campaign criteria.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for creators mentioning your brand or product keywords">
    Enter your brand name, product name, or relevant keywords. Syncly surfaces creators whose video content — including spoken audio captured by Audio Intelligence — references these terms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review creator profiles with Syncly's AI analysis">
    Click into any creator profile to see the full AI analysis: audience demographics, engagement quality breakdown, brand alignment score, top-performing content, and recent brand mentions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save shortlisted creators to a campaign">
    Add promising creators to a campaign directly from their profile. Shortlisted creators flow into Campaign Tracking automatically, where you can manage outreach and monitor performance once a partnership is live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Creator Profile View

Each creator profile in Syncly Social gives you a consolidated view of everything you need to make an informed partnership decision:

- **Recent brand mentions** — the specific videos and posts where the creator has referenced your brand or related keywords, with timestamps and platform links
- **Engagement rate and trend** — current engagement rate alongside a historical trend line so you can see whether their audience is growing more or less active
- **Top-performing content** — their highest-engagement posts, giving you a clear signal of what their audience responds to
- **Audience demographic breakdown** — age ranges, top geographies, and interest categories of their followers
- **Brand alignment score** — Syncly's AI assessment of how well the creator fits your brand, with a breakdown of the factors driving the score
- **Platform presence** — which platforms the creator is active on and where their engagement is strongest

## Exporting a Creator List

Once you've shortlisted creators, you can export your list as a structured report for sharing with stakeholders or agency partners. The export includes key metrics for each creator — reach, engagement rate, brand alignment score, and recent mention data — formatted for use in campaign planning decks or vendor briefings.

To export, go to your shortlist, click **Export List**, and choose your preferred format.

<Tip>
  Prioritize creators with a high engagement rate over those with a high raw follower count. A creator with 50,000 highly engaged followers in your target demographic will almost always outperform a creator with 500,000 passive followers. Syncly's brand alignment score and engagement quality metrics are your most reliable filters for identifying creators who will actually move the needle.
</Tip>
