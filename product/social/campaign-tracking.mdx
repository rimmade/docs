Running an influencer campaign across multiple creators and platforms means tracking dozens of moving pieces at once. Syncly Social's Campaign Tracking feature puts that entire process on autopilot. Once you set up a campaign, Syncly monitors every piece of creator content — including video audio and on-screen visuals — across all active platforms and consolidates performance data into a single dashboard. You stay focused on strategy while Syncly handles the tracking.

## What Campaign Tracking Does

Campaign Tracking gives you automated, continuous monitoring of influencer campaigns from launch through completion. It tracks reach, engagement, mention volume, and sentiment for every creator in your campaign, across every platform they post on, and surfaces top-performing content in real time.

This replaces the spreadsheet-and-manual-check workflow that most teams rely on — a process that's slow, error-prone, and impossible to scale across large creator rosters. With Syncly, your campaign data is always current, always complete, and always in one place.

## The Autopilot Model

Once you configure a campaign in Syncly Social, tracking runs automatically. You don't need to manually check creator accounts, pull performance reports, or reconcile data from multiple platforms. Syncly's Audio Intelligence monitors video content for spoken brand mentions, AI Vision tracks visual brand appearances, and the platform integrations capture text-based engagement data — all without any action required from your team after setup.

This is especially valuable for large campaigns with many creators posting across different platforms and time zones. Syncly tracks all of it in parallel, in real time.

## Setting Up a Campaign

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Campaigns in Syncly Social">
    Log in at [creator.syncly.app](https://creator.syncly.app) and navigate to the Campaigns section from the main sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New Campaign">
    Select **New Campaign** to open the campaign setup flow. You can create multiple campaigns simultaneously to track different product launches, partnerships, or brand initiatives separately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name the campaign and set the date range">
    Give your campaign a clear name and define its start and end dates. Setting an accurate date range ensures your performance data is scoped correctly and pre-campaign baseline data remains separate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add influencers and creators">
    Add the creators participating in the campaign. You can pull directly from your Creator Discovery shortlist or add creators manually by entering their handles. Syncly will begin monitoring their accounts across all connected platforms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add tracking keywords, hashtags, and mentions">
    Enter the campaign hashtags, brand mentions, product keywords, and any custom tracking terms you want Syncly to monitor. These signals help capture content that references the campaign even when creators don't follow a strict posting brief.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable autopilot tracking">
    Turn on autopilot to activate continuous monitoring. From this point forward, Syncly tracks all campaign content automatically and updates your dashboard as new posts and videos go live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Campaign Dashboard

Your campaign dashboard gives you a real-time view of campaign performance across all creators and platforms:

- **Total reach** — the combined audience reached by all creator posts in the campaign, updated as new content is published.
- **Engagement** — aggregate likes, comments, shares, and saves across all campaign content, with a breakdown by creator and platform.
- **Mention volume** — how many times your brand, product, or campaign keywords have been mentioned — including spoken mentions captured by Audio Intelligence.
- **Sentiment** — the overall sentiment trend for campaign content, showing whether audience response is positive, neutral, or negative, and how that shifts over the campaign period.
- **Top-performing content** — the individual posts and videos generating the highest engagement, surfaced automatically so your team can amplify and repurpose the best-performing assets.

## Comparing Campaign Performance Across Creators

The creator comparison view lets you evaluate each influencer's contribution to the campaign side by side. For each creator you'll see their individual reach, engagement rate, mention volume, and sentiment score. This makes it straightforward to identify which creators are delivering the strongest results and which partnerships may need adjustment mid-campaign.

Use this view to inform future creator selection and campaign budget allocation with concrete performance data rather than estimates.

## Generating a Campaign Report

When your campaign ends — or at any point during it — you can generate a full campaign report directly from the dashboard. The report includes aggregate campaign metrics, per-creator performance breakdowns, top content highlights, sentiment analysis, and a timeline of campaign activity.

To generate a report, open your campaign dashboard, click **Generate Report**, and select your preferred format. Reports are structured for sharing with internal stakeholders, clients, or agency partners.

<Tip>
  Run a pre-campaign baseline measurement before your campaign launches. Set up your tracking keywords and brand monitoring at least one to two weeks before the campaign start date to capture your brand's organic mention volume and sentiment at rest. This gives you a clear before-and-after comparison that demonstrates the true lift your campaign delivered.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Syncly Social's Audio Intelligence captures spoken brand mentions in creator videos — including posts that don't include your official campaign hashtag. If a creator mentions your brand name out loud but doesn't use the hashtag in their caption, Syncly still tracks it. This significantly improves mention coverage in campaigns where creator posting behavior varies from the brief.
</Note>
