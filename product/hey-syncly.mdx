Hey Syncly is Syncly's built-in AI chat interface that lets you ask plain-English questions about your customer feedback and receive instant, data-backed answers — no dashboards to build, no filters to configure, no SQL required. Whether you want a quick pulse check on sentiment or a deep dive into a specific customer segment, Hey Syncly searches across all your connected feedback sources in real time and surfaces exactly what you need.

## Accessing Hey Syncly

To open Hey Syncly, click the **chat bubble icon** in the bottom-right corner of any Syncly page, or select **Hey Syncly** from the left sidebar navigation. The chat panel slides open without leaving your current view, so you can ask questions in context while reviewing a dashboard or report.

## What You Can Ask

Hey Syncly understands natural language, so you can write questions the same way you'd phrase them to a colleague. The more context you give — time range, data source, customer segment — the more precise your answer will be.

## How Hey Syncly Finds Answers

When you submit a question, Hey Syncly searches across every feedback source you've connected to Syncly — including support tickets, app store reviews, NPS surveys, CRM notes, and more. It identifies relevant signals, applies sentiment and topic classification, and composes a plain-language answer with supporting data points drawn directly from your ingested feedback.

Hey Syncly does not rely on generic knowledge or external internet sources. Every answer is grounded in your own customer data, so results are specific to your product, your customers, and your configured integrations.

## Tips for Writing Effective Questions

Getting the best answers from Hey Syncly is straightforward — the key is being specific. Consider including:

- **A time range** — "this month," "last quarter," "since March 1st," or "the past 30 days" help Hey Syncly narrow results to the period you care about.
- **A data source** — specify "in Zendesk tickets," "from app store reviews," or "in NPS responses" if you want to isolate a particular channel.
- **A customer segment** — "enterprise customers," "free-tier users," or "customers in EMEA" sharpen the focus of any answer.
- **A specific topic or feature** — ask about "checkout," "onboarding," or "mobile app" to zoom in on a product area.

**Effective:** "What checkout-related issues did enterprise customers report in Zendesk tickets last quarter?"

**Less effective:** "What are people saying?"

You can also ask follow-up questions in the same conversation thread. Hey Syncly maintains context within a session, so you can refine or pivot your inquiry without starting over.

## What Hey Syncly Can and Cannot Do

Hey Syncly is a powerful companion to your Syncly workspace, but it's designed to complement — not replace — your dashboards and reports.

**Hey Syncly can:**
- Answer ad-hoc questions about trends, themes, and sentiment across any connected source
- Compare metrics across time periods, segments, or feedback channels
- Surface specific examples and representative feedback quotes to support its answers
- Help you quickly explore a hypothesis before building a formal dashboard or report

**Hey Syncly cannot:**
- Answer questions about data that has not yet been ingested into Syncly — if a source isn't connected or a sync hasn't completed, that data won't appear in responses
- Replace the full analytical depth of a configured dashboard with filters, saved views, and historical breakdowns
- Make business decisions on your behalf — use its answers as a starting point for investigation, not a final conclusion
- Access data outside your Syncly workspace or pull from external sources

<Note>
Hey Syncly's answers reflect your most recently synced data. If a feedback source completed its last sync several hours ago, responses won't include feedback received since that sync. Check the **Data Sources** page to view sync timestamps and trigger a manual sync if you need the latest data.
</Note>
