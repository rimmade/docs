This quickstart walks you through the essential steps to go from a fresh Syncly account to your first real customer insight. By the end, you'll have data flowing in, AI-generated tags and sentiment scores applied automatically, and a dashboard ready to share with your team. Each step links to a deeper guide if you want to explore further.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    If you haven't signed up yet, head to [syncly.app](https://syncly.app) and click **Get a Demo** or **Start Free**. Fill in your company details, verify your email address, and sign in. Enterprise teams can configure single sign-on (SSO) after the initial setup.

    → Full instructions: [Create Your Syncly Account](/getting-started/create-account)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Up Your Workspace">
    After signing in, Syncly walks you through a brief workspace setup. Give your workspace a name, set your timezone, and choose a default language. These settings affect how Syncly groups feedback by time and how it presents trending analysis, so take a moment to get them right.

    → Full instructions: [Set Up Your Workspace](/getting-started/workspace)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Your First Data Source">
    Navigate to **Settings → Integrations** and connect the tool where most of your customer feedback lives today. Syncly supports 19+ integrations across three categories:

    | Category | Example Integrations |
    |---|---|
    | Support & Helpdesk | Zendesk, Intercom, Front, Gorgias, HelpDesk, Atlas |
    | CRM & Surveys | Salesforce, HubSpot, SurveyMonkey, Typeform |
    | Calls, Messaging & Import | Gong, Grain, Slack, Discord, Gmail, Excel/CSV |

    Select your integration, authenticate with your existing credentials, and choose which data to sync. Syncly begins importing your feedback immediately. If you don't have an integration ready, upload a CSV file to get started right away.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore Your Feedback in the Feedback Hub">
    Once your first sync completes, open the **Feedback Hub** from the left sidebar. You'll see your customer feedback already organized and tagged by Syncly's AI — no setup required. Use the filter bar at the top to narrow feedback by:

    - **Channel** — filter to a specific integration or data source
    - **Sentiment** — view positive, neutral, or negative feedback
    - **Tag** — drill into a specific topic or category
    - **Date range** — focus on a recent period or compare time windows

    Take a few minutes to browse the tags Syncly has applied. You can refine any auto-generated tag or add custom tags to match your team's taxonomy.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ask Hey Syncly Your First Question">
    Click **Hey Syncly** in the left sidebar to open the AI chat. Hey Syncly lets you ask any question about your customer feedback in plain language and returns an immediate, cited answer drawn from your data.

    Try starting with a question like:

    > *"What are customers complaining about most this month?"*

    Or explore other starter questions:

    - *"Which product area has the most negative sentiment in the last 30 days?"*
    - *"Summarize the top three themes from Zendesk tickets this quarter."*
    - *"How has sentiment around our onboarding changed over the past 60 days?"*

    Hey Syncly cites the specific feedback items behind each answer, so you can verify every insight and dig deeper with a follow-up question.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Your First Dashboard">
    Open **Dashboards** from the left sidebar and click **New Dashboard**. Give your dashboard a name — for example, "Weekly Customer Health" or "Support Trends Q3" — and add your first widgets:

    1. Choose a widget type (trend chart, sentiment breakdown, topic volume, or feedback list).
    2. Apply any filters relevant to your audience (channel, date range, customer segment).
    3. Arrange your widgets on the canvas by dragging and dropping.
    4. Click **Share** to send a live link to stakeholders or export a one-click report to PDF.

    Your dashboard updates automatically as new feedback flows in, so it stays relevant without any manual refreshing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Invite your teammates to Syncly before you start building dashboards. When colleagues have their own accounts, you can assign roles, share dashboards directly within the app, and collaborate on tag taxonomies together. Head to **Settings → Team → Invite** to send invitations. → [Invite Your Team](/getting-started/invite-team)
</Tip>
