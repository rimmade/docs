## What Is Syncly?

Syncly is an AI-native customer intelligence suite built for modern, fast-growing brands. It brings together two powerful products under one roof:

**Syncly (Feedback Intelligence)** — Syncly automatically collects customer feedback from every channel, organizes it with AI-powered tagging and sentiment analysis, and surfaces the insights your team needs to act with confidence. Whether your feedback lives in support tickets, CRM conversations, surveys, or call recordings, Syncly brings it all into one place and makes it immediately useful.

**Syncly Social (Video Social Listening)** — Syncly Social extends your customer intelligence to the social web. It monitors video content and social platforms to capture what customers are saying about your brand, your competitors, and your market — giving you a real-time pulse on the conversations that matter most.

Together, these two products give you a complete picture of the customer voice, from private support interactions to public social conversations.

## The Syncly Workflow

Every workflow in Syncly follows four core stages. Understanding these stages helps you get the most out of every feature:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Collect">
    Connect Syncly to the tools where your customer feedback already lives — support platforms like Zendesk, Intercom, and Gorgias; CRMs like Salesforce and HubSpot; survey tools like Typeform and SurveyMonkey; call recorders like Gong and Grain; and more. Syncly pulls feedback in automatically, so your data is always fresh and complete. You can also import data manually via Excel or CSV.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize">
    Once your feedback is collected, Syncly's AI engine goes to work. It automatically applies tags, detects sentiment, and categorizes every piece of feedback by topic, channel, and customer segment — without any manual effort from your team. The result is a structured, searchable Feedback Hub that replaces chaotic inboxes and disconnected spreadsheets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Discover">
    With your feedback organized, you can start finding what matters. Syncly surfaces trending topics as they emerge, highlights sentiment shifts across segments, and lets you ask freeform questions through Hey Syncly, the built-in AI chat. Cross-analysis lets you compare feedback patterns across time periods, channels, customer tiers, and more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Act">
    Insights are only valuable when they drive action. Syncly helps you act on what you learn through one-click reports, automated Smart Briefs delivered to Slack or email, dashboard sharing, and direct integrations with tools like Jira and Linear so you can turn customer pain points into product tasks instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
